About Me

Coming from the retail world, I felt like I had room to grow, and tackle a new skillset. Since then, I found I fit well into programming; it's incredibly fun to sit down and tinker with a problem. I've learned so much since, and I'm here to help you with your web presence.

Why I'm Here

Covid has impacted how we do business. I've seen several local shops around me struggle to stay afloat. But that doesn't need to be the case. Now, more than ever, an online presence has become necessary. I believe in a world where we can overcome and thrive despite the obstacles life throws at us.

What I Do

First, we talk, and I learn exactly what your goals are. Then, I draft a contract for us to know exactly what I'm going to get done. From there, I set out and build your project, while giving you weekly updates along the way.
